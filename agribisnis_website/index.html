Welcome to the official website of the Agribusiness Study Program, Faculty of Science and Technology, UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta.

This website is here to introduce the Agribusiness Study Program to the entire community without space and time limitations. We hope that this website can become a source of information so that we can receive feedback from the wider community.

Tentang Prodi Lihat Kurikulum

Vision

" To become a study programme that is nationally superior and intentionally recognised in the field of urban agribusiness through Islamic and Indonesian foundations."

Mission

  • Carrying out education and teaching in the field of urban agribusiness to produce scholars who meet global excellence and competitiveness
  • Providing a moral and spiritual foundation for the development of science and engineering in urban agribusiness
  • Conducting research in the field of urban agribusiness that is beneficial to increasing community welfare
  • Improving the quality of life and development of the society, specifically agribusiness actors, through community services
